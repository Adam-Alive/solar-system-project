Our Cruises

Inner Planets Cruise (7 Nights)

The tour begins with spectacular orbits around the Earth and then takes in Venus and Mercury, with one day devoted to sun-watching. The return leg sweeps by the red planet, Mars, with a gentle return to our own blue planet.

Outer Planets Cruise (14 Nights)

After safely traversing the asteroid belt you will experience the magnificence of the four outer 'giant planets' namely Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus and Neptune. Gaze in wonder at Saturn's rings and Jupiter's moons and ponder the ice-worlds of Uranus and Neptune. You will also see Pluto from afar - technically a dwarf planet since 2006.

Solar System Cruise (21 Nights)

A fully immersive space experience, you will encounter all eight planets as highlighted above with plenty of time for rest, relaxation and indulgence on this trip of a lifetime.
